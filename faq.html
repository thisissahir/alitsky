Getting found: Google, local, and AI search

We build your site so Google can read every page and understand your service area, which is the foundation ranking is built on. We do not promise a specific position, because no honest company can. What we control is the structure, speed, and clarity that give you the best chance to rank and to get found.

More customers now ask tools like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity for recommendations instead of scrolling links. If those tools cannot understand your business, they recommend a competitor and you never know you lost the job. We build your site so AI tools can clearly read who you are, what you do, and where.

GEO stands for generative engine optimization. It means structuring your website and content so AI search tools can find, trust, and cite your business when someone asks for a recommendation. It is the new layer on top of traditional search, and most local businesses have not done it yet.

AEO stands for answer engine optimization. It means building your website so that when someone asks a question, your business shows up as the direct answer, like the answer box at the top of Google or what a voice assistant reads out loud. In plain terms, it helps you become the answer people get, instead of just one more link they have to dig through.

They are closely related, and every build does both. AEO (answer engine optimization) is about being the direct answer to a question, like the box at the top of Google or what a voice assistant reads back. GEO (generative engine optimization) is about being found, trusted, and recommended inside AI tools like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity when they write an answer for someone. The simplest way to think about it: AEO helps you win the quick answer, and GEO helps you get named when an AI makes a recommendation.

That is exactly what the build is structured for.
