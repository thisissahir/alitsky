AI SEO & GEO

The search engine has changed. Has your business?

People stopped Googling. They started asking AI.

Something shifted in the last two years and most businesses have not noticed yet. Your customers are no longer typing into Google and scrolling through ten blue links. They are opening ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, or their phone's AI assistant and asking a question: "Who is the best electrician in Denver?" or "Find me a reliable accountant near me." And the AI gives them one answer. Maybe two. Not a page of results. One name. If that name is not yours, the job is gone before you even knew it existed.

The new layer

What is GEO?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization: the practice of making sure your business gets recommended by AI systems, not just ranked on Google. It is the new frontier of being found, and almost no local businesses are doing it yet. That is the advantage.

What we do

Both layers. Done properly.

Traditional SEO

Your website ranks on Google for the searches that still happen there. Keywords, structure, speed, backlinks, local listings. Done properly.

GEO optimization

We structure your content, reviews, business data, and online presence so that AI systems cite and recommend you when customers ask for exactly what you offer.

Google Business Profile

Fully optimised, updated, and working as a signal for both Google and AI systems.

Local citation building

Your business information is consistent and accurate everywhere it appears online.

Monthly reporting

You see exactly where you rank, what is improving, and what AI systems are saying about your business.

Why now

The window is smaller this time.

The businesses that do this now will own their category in their city. The businesses that wait will spend years trying to catch up. This is the same moment that existed when websites first became important. Except the window is smaller this time.
