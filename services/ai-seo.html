What is GEO?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization. It is the practice of making sure your business gets recommended by AI systems, not just ranked on Google.

Traditional SEO gets you on page one of Google. GEO gets you into the answer when someone asks an AI assistant what the best option is. These are different things. Both matter. GEO is the one almost nobody has done yet.

How GEO actually works

Four steps. Then a monthly rhythm.

STEP 01

Unlock the crawlers

GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot. Most sites block them by default. We open the door so AI can find you.

STEP 02

Speak AI's language

Schema markup, Bing submission, answer-first content. AI cannot recommend what it cannot read. We make you readable.

STEP 03

Enter the index

ChatGPT sources from Bing. Most businesses are not there. We submit, verify, and confirm your presence.

STEP 04

Monitor every month

We test your top queries every month. Track appearance. Adjust. Report. GEO is not set-and-forget. And neither are we.
