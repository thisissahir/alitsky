Automations

Stop doing the work that should not need you.

Automate the repetitive. Focus on the irreplaceable.

There are things in your business that only you can do — the expertise, the judgment, the relationships. And then there are things that anyone could do if they had the time: following up with leads, sending invoices, requesting reviews, chasing no-shows, updating spreadsheets. We eliminate the second category so you can own the first.

What we automate

Seven quiet wins.

Quote to invoice

A job is finished, the invoice goes out automatically. No chasing, no forgetting.

Lead follow-up

Someone fills out a form or calls and does not book. An automated message goes out within minutes. Most jobs are won by whoever responds first.

Review requests

After every completed job, your happy customer gets a message asking for a Google review. Your reputation builds itself.

Appointment reminders

No-shows drop. Your schedule stays full.

New enquiry routing

Leads go to the right person, the right system, the right next step. Nothing falls through a crack.

Reporting & dashboards

The numbers you need, where you need them, without pulling data manually every week.

CRM buildouts

Your contacts, jobs, and client history organised in one place that actually makes sense.

The result

Your Sundays stop being catch-up days.
