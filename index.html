Common questions

Answers, in plain English.

What is an AI-ready website?

It is a website built so Google, maps, and AI tools can clearly understand who you are, what you do, where you work, and why a customer should trust you. That makes your business easier to find and easier to evaluate.

How does it help my business?

More of your customers now search on their phones and ask AI tools for recommendations. A site built for this is easier to find, loads fast, and makes the next step obvious: call, book, or ask for help. That gives more visitors a clear path to become booked jobs.

What is included in the audit?

A plain-English review of where your website loses customers before they call, a check of whether AI search tools can understand your business, a competitor snapshot, and a prioritized fix list. Delivered in 2 to 3 business days with a short video walkthrough.

Why does the audit say $197 and $397?

The standard audit is $397. Founding clients receive the audit for $197 while we build our first documented HVAC and home service case studies.

Do I own the website after it is built?

Yes. You own the domain, the code, the hosting project, and every account, in writing. We hold access to help you, never to lock you in.

How long does a custom website take?

Most builds take 10 to 21 business days from the time we have your information and access.

Does this work for plumbing, roofing, and other trades?

Yes. We are starting with HVAC because we want to prove the work in one trade first. The same approach fits plumbing, roofing, electrical, and other home services.
